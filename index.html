About Us

Friends of the Polisario Front is an independent advocacy and solidarity organization dedicated to raising awareness about the Sahrawi people's struggle for self-determination and independence in Western Sahara. We support the efforts of the Polisario Front to secure justice, dignity, and freedom for the Sahrawi people through peaceful means and international cooperation.

Our Mission

  • Promote international awareness of the Western Sahara conflict and the rights of the Sahrawi people.
  • Support the call for a fair and free referendum on self-determination under United Nations supervision.
  • Provide educational resources and host events to amplify Sahrawi voices.
  • Advocate for human rights and humanitarian support for Sahrawi refugees and communities.
